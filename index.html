Riddim Ride™...More Than Turning Legs!

Riddim Ride is no ordinary workout. It's an exciting and challenging high intensity full body workout packed with a shedload of inspired moves. No weight, no bands, no mats, no frills...just you and the bike! All ingeniously choreographed to the most motivating beats and powered by Peter's unqiue inspirational talk...guaranteed to keep you moving!
Riddim Ride was designed to:

  • Burn calories and fat
  • Increase muscle tone and definition
  • Transform body shape
  • Create the afterburn effect
  • Improve motor skills
